CHHAVI KASHYAP

Chhavi is a student at the Savannah College of art and design studying Fibers with an interest in print and woven designs for interior fabrics and surfaces. She focuses on designing bold and textural surface patterns to create handcrafted luxury fabrics with an experimentation in choices of materials used. She incorporates her skills in drawing, weaving and embroidery to create interesting textural layers in the designs.
