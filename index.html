CHHAVI KASHYAP

Born and raised in India, my connection with textiles is innate. Travel experiences across states within the country and learning different craft techniques not only helped me build my relationship with textiles but also gave me the opportunity to explore a variety of materials and experience the process of making. Patience and skills learnt from these craft practices perfectly defines my identity as a maker.

I work with a variety of elements, mostly organic objects, often picked on my way to different destinations. The materials lend a unique surface quality to my work. Through my pieces, I attempt to achieve a combination of textural variety and boldness of forms,a juxtaposition of complexity and simplicity.

With my concepts and inspiration, I tend to move back and forth between the discipline followed by age-old craft techniques and asymmetry of the contemporary practices, balancing myself at the combination of the two. My work aims to create textile print, patterns and surfaces that are industry production friendly with unique and unconventional accents.
